Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Kiana Realty Growth Platform

1. Product Overview

Product Name: Kiana Realty Growth Platform
Prepared For: Kiana Realty (Rushikesh)
Built & Operated By: Insignia Consultancy Solutions

Vision

Build a premium, tech-enabled real estate operating system that:

Feels effortless and aspirational for buyers
Is fully measurable and controllable for the business
Enables lean scaling without heavy upfront hiring 2. Problem Statement

Current real estate operations suffer from:

Lead leakage due to manual tracking
Fragmented workflows across calls, WhatsApp, spreadsheets
Poor visibility into pipeline, visits, and conversions
High dependency on human coordination
Inefficient use of marketing spend

Kiana wants to:

Avoid traditional high-headcount brokerage model
Build a premium brand experience
Operate via a lean, tech-first system 3. Product Goals
Primary Goals

Enable lean operations with fewer hires
Build long-term business memory (data asset)
