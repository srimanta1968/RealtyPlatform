1 · Phase Goal

Until Phase 5 the buyer experiences premium only when interacting with humans. Phase 5 makes the digital surface feel premium too — an authenticated portal with shortlists, comparisons, documents, and a refined recommender.

In Scope
Buyer self-serve
Customer Portal (Next.js) · Document Service · e-sign integration · Recommendation Agent v2 · communication history view · payment receipt tracking · notification preferences.
Out of Scope
Deferred
Concierge AI / chat (P6) · full mortgage workflow · title/registration · native mobile customer app.

7 · Recommendation Agent v2

Agent · Phase 5 (Upgrade)

Portal-aware Recommendations

Builds on Phase 4 Property Matching with new signals: portal views, dwell time, save/unsave, comparison sets, and last-discussed property from call notes.

Surfaces in: Customer Portal ("You might also like") and Presales Cockpit ("Suggested next conversation").

Explainability: every suggestion shows reason — same bar as Phase 4.

8 · Trust & Security

  • Customer auth: phone OTP (primary), magic-link email (secondary), passkey (optional)
  • Document encryption at rest with per-tenant keys
  • Watermark customer-downloaded brochures with their name + timestamp
  • ACL audit log on every document access
  • Customer can request data export (DPDP compliance)
  • Right-to-erasure workflow

11 · Exit Criteria

  • ≥50% of QUALIFIED-stage leads activate their portal account
  • ≥30% of activated customers maintain a shortlist
  • Document verification turnaround <24h for ≥90% of uploads
  • Token-to-booking cycle time reduced by ≥20% vs Phase 4
  • Customer NPS for portal ≥30 in first month
