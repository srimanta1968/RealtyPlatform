1 · Phase Goal

Phase 2 produced clean funnel data. Phase 3 turns that data into outbound growth: segment by intent and behavior, run drip and broadcast campaigns across email/WA/SMS, score lead warmth from engagement, and push warm leads into a call queue presales works first. First LLM agents go live.

In Scope

Campaign Service · Segmentation Engine · Engagement Tracker · Marketing Console (React) · Lead Qualification Agent (LLM) · Campaign Scoring Agent · A/B framework.
Out of Scope
Deferred
Field PWA (P4) · Customer Portal (P5) · Concierge (P6) · outbound voice / dialer.

5 · LLM Agents

Agent · Phase 3

Lead Qualification (LLM)

Trigger: lead.created with free-text enquiry, or any inbound message.

Extracts: intent · budget band · location preference · urgency · per-field confidence.

Guardrails: confidence threshold gates auto-stage moves; below threshold goes to human review queue. Reasoning stored for audit.

Agent · Phase 3

Campaign Scoring

Trigger: any engagement.* event.

Logic: rule-based first (recency × frequency × intent), upgrade to gradient-boosted model in late P3.

Output: lead.scored with components for explainability; warm queue resorted.

8 · Deliverability & Compliance

  • DKIM/SPF/DMARC for email
  • WhatsApp opt-in proof captured at lead source
  • Suppression list shared across channels
  • DPDP-compliant consent capture and revocation
  • Per-recipient throttling to protect sender reputation

11 · Exit Criteria

  • Cost per qualified lead measurable, trending down month-over-month
  • ≥30% of presales daily call volume comes from warm queue
  • Auto-qualification covers ≥60% of inbound leads with confidence ≥0.7
  • At least one campaign completes a full attribution cycle to closure
  • Open + click rates within industry benchmark; bounce <2%
