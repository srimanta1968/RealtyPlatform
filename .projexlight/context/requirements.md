# Requirements - Quick Prototype Sprint

## Project: Kiana Realty Growth Platform

A Kiana-branded growth platform combining a resort-style property website, CRM, site-visit operations, field-team app, customer communication, marketing automation and AI-native workflows — operated by Insignia as a customer-success partner until Kiana chooses to bring more of the process in-house.

## Sprint Overview

Quick prototype sprint for generated project structure

## Epics

### Customer Experience Layer

A comprehensive layer focused on enhancing customer interactions and lead management through user-friendly interfaces and structured workflows.

## Features

### User Registration

Facilitate user sign-ups with essential details, ensuring a smooth onboarding process.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
["Users can register using email and password.","User registration form validates input fields correctly.","Confirmation email is sent after successful registration.","Users can log in using registered credentials."]

### Lead Capture and Management

System for capturing inbound leads and converting them into structured workflows.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
["Leads can be captured from multiple sources.","Leads are stored in a structured format.","Users can view and manage leads in the system."]

### Core Business Workflow

Establish the primary business logic for tracking user interactions and conversions.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
["Core business workflows are defined and can be executed.","Users can see conversion metrics from leads to customers.","The system captures necessary data for future reference."]

### Data CRUD Operations

Create basic functionality for users to perform CRUD operations on their data.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
["Users can create, read, update, and delete records for leads and workflows.","Data integrity is maintained through operations.","Users receive feedback after each operation."]
